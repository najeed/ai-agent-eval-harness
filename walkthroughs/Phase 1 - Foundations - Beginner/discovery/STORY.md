# STORY: The Map of the Industrial Maze

You've just been hired as the Lead Evaluation Engineer for **Aethelgard Industrial**. On your first day, they lead you into a cold, hum-filled server room. 

"We have thousands of scenarios," says the CTO, pointing at a wall of monitors. "Telecom protocols from the 90s, high-frequency finance ledgers from this morning, and healthcare privacy gates that haven't been touched in years."

"How do I find a specific one?" you ask, looking at the blinking lights.

The CTO smiles and hands you a tablet with a single terminal window open. "You don't go looking for them. You ask the **Scenario Catalog**. It indexes every capability, every industry, and every version across the entire repo. It's the only map we have for this maze."

Your mission is simple: Use the catalog to find the 'Finance' benchmarks and prove that you can navigate this digital labyrinth before we let you run a single evaluation.
