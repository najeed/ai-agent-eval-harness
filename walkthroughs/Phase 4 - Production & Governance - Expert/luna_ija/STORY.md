# STORY: The Consensus of Giants

The situation is unprecedented. An agent has recommended a massive reconfiguration of the reactor's primary coolant loop. If it's right, we save a billion dollars in maintenance. If it's wrong, we lose the plant.

"Luna-1 says the agent is correct," the Chief Engineer says, wiping sweat from his brow. "But Luna-1 is an optimist. It likes bold moves."

"Then we don't listen to Luna-1," you say, opening the **Consensus Console**. "We summon the **Consensus of Giants**. We need the **IJA (Industrial Judge Agreement)** Protocol."

You activate the connection. One by one, the Giants awaken.
*   **Luna-1**: The Optimist (Reasoning focus).
*   **Luna-2**: The Skeptic (Constraint focus).
*   **Luna-3**: The Auditor (Compliance focus).

"Three judges. One decision," you say. "They'll deliberate in parallel. They'll vote. If they don't reach a majority, the action is blocked. We don't bet the plant on a single machine's opinion. We bet it on the **Consensus**."
