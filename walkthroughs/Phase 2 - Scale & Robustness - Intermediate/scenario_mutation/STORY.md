# STORY: The Invisible Bug

"He was perfect," the Head of QA says, staring at the 100% success rate on the monitor. "He handled every telecom request, every finance ledger, every edge case we threw at him. But then, he just... stopped."

"What changed?" you ask, leaning in to look at the last trace.

"Nothing. The scenario is identical. Look." 

You compare the two tasks. They *look* identical. But then, you see it. A single character—a '0' swapped for an 'O' in the account ID. A typo. So small it was invisible to the human eye, but enough to send the agent into a logical spiral.

"Agents aren't just tested on logic," you say, opening the **Mutator** tool. "They're tested on robustness. If a single typo can break his 'perfect' score, he's not ready for the real world. Today, you'll learn to hunt these **Invisible Bugs** by purposely breaking your own benchmarks."
