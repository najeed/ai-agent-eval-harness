# 🏠 Housing & Infrastructure Guide
High-fidelity housing data extraction for AI agents.

## Data Sources
- **HUD**: U.S. Housing and Urban Development.
- **World Bank**: Global infrastructure indicators.

## Schema
- `location`: MSA or Country.
- `year`: Observation year.
- `rent_2br`: Fair market rent for 2-bedroom units.
- `access_rate`: Percentage (e.g., electricity access).
