# 🎬 Media & Entertainment Guide

High-fidelity media consumption and cultural trend data extraction for AI agents. This sector powers recommendation and trend analyzer bots.

## Status: **HARDENED**
- **Architecture**: IMDb Dataset integration.
- **Verification**: 100% Parity.

## Data Sources
- **IMDb**: Global film and television ratings, metadata, and cast rosters.
- **Spotify**: Cultural audio trends, genre popularity, and track metadata (Simulated).

## 🛠️ Schema (`StandardSchema`)
- `title`: Content identifier (Movie, Show, or Track).
- `creator`: Primary creator or performer.
- `metric`: `rating_score`, `total_streams`, `popularity_index`, or `award_count`.
- `value`: Numerical success metric.
